Rakhine Mote Ti

Description

Often referred to as ar-pu shar-pu (hot throat, hot tongue) and as the name suggests, this dish of rice noodles and fish soup packs a punch. A perfect way to warm your cockles during the winter. It makes me think of mohingar as there are similarities, yet it has its own distinct taste.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Water
  • Mote Phat
  • Fish Cake
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare hot water
  2. Boil Your fish
  3. Add seasoning to your boiled fish soup.
  4. Add Fish sauce to your boiled fish soup.
  5. Add adequate amount of fish soup and mote phat in a bowl and add sliced fish cake over it.
